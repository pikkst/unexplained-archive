### For Investigators and Administrators

#### Translating a Case:
1. **Open a case** - Click on any case to view details
2. **Find translation panel** - At the top of case details, you'll see "Translation Tool" panel (if you have permission)
3. **Select language** - Choose target language from dropdown (e.g., English, Estonian, Spanish, etc.)
4. **Click "Translate Case"** - Entire case (title, description, detailed report) will be translated
5. **View result** - Translated text appears with blue badge "Translated to..."
6. **Toggle back** - Click "Show Original" to see original text

#### Translating Comments:
1. **Scroll to comments section**
2. **Find a comment** in a foreign language
3. **Click "Translate"** button below the comment
4. **View translation** - Comment now shows translated version
5. **Original text** - Click "Show original" to revert

#### Setting Preferred Language:
1. Go to your **profile page**
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Scroll to **"Preferred Language"** section
4. **Select your language** (e.g., Estonian, Russian, Spanish)
5. Click **"Save Changes"**
6. System will now default to this language for translations

### For Regular Users (without translation rights)
- You see cases in their original language
- You cannot translate cases (this is for investigators/admins only)
- If you want to submit your case in another language, check "Auto-translate for AI" on submission form
